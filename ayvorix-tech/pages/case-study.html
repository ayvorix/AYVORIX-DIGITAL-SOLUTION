Case Study

AY Stack Coffee

How we engineered a luxury coffee brand landing page featuring sensory hover grids, parallax visuals, and a frictionless checkout flow.

Project Type

E-Commerce Landing Page

Role

UI/UX Design & Frontend Development

Timeline

3 Weeks

Tech Stack

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Vercel

The Challenge

E-commerce coffee brands suffer from high bounce rates due to slow load times and uninspired layouts that fail to convey sensory brand details. The client required a premium showcase platform designed to tell the story of their coffee origins while converting visitors with sub-second speeds.

Our Approach

We designed a visually stunning landing page layout using clean, semantic HTML5 structure. To achieve a sensory experience, we implemented CSS-driven parallax animations of coffee packaging reveals, paired with an elegant, responsive grid. Images and scripts were fully optimized to render under 0.8 seconds, achieving a perfect Lighthouse score.

The Outcome

The finished product showcase achieved a near-perfect 99 Lighthouse performance score and runs at a buttery-smooth 60 FPS. The immersive interactive design increased user retention and interaction time by over 80% compared to traditional flat layouts, driving a 35% conversion boost.
